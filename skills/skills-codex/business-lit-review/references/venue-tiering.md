# Business Venue Tiering

Use these tiers as search and positioning guides. They are priorities, not universal quality claims.

## Accounting

### Core Accounting Journals

- `The Accounting Review (TAR)`
- `Journal of Accounting Research (JAR)`
- `Journal of Accounting and Economics (JAE)`
- `Review of Accounting Studies (RAS)`
- `Contemporary Accounting Research (CAR)`

### Other Strong Accounting Outlets

- `Accounting, Organizations and Society (AOS)`
- `Auditing: A Journal of Practice & Theory`
- `Journal of Accounting, Auditing & Finance`
- `Journal of Business Finance & Accounting`

## Finance

### Core Finance Journals

- `Journal of Finance (JF)`
- `Journal of Financial Economics (JFE)`
- `Review of Financial Studies (RFS)`

### Other Strong Finance Outlets

- `Review of Finance`
- `Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis (JFQA)`
- `Journal of Corporate Finance`
- `Financial Management`

## Management and Organizations

### Core Management Journals

- `Administrative Science Quarterly (ASQ)`
- `Academy of Management Journal (AMJ)`
- `Academy of Management Review (AMR)`
- `Strategic Management Journal (SMJ)`
- `Management Science`
- `Organization Science`

### Adjacent Strong Outlets

- `Journal of Management`
- `Journal of International Business Studies`
- `Research Policy`
- `Information Systems Research`
- `MIS Quarterly`

## Economics

### General Interest

- `American Economic Review (AER)`
- `Quarterly Journal of Economics (QJE)`
- `Journal of Political Economy (JPE)`
- `Review of Economic Studies (REStud)`
- `Econometrica`

### Field Journals Often Relevant

- `Journal of Public Economics`
- `Journal of Labor Economics`
- `Journal of Monetary Economics`
- `RAND Journal of Economics`
- `Review of Economics and Statistics`

## Escalation Rule

Start from the target field. Expand to adjacent fields when:

- the construct originates elsewhere
- the identification design is standard in another field
- the target journal conversation cites that adjacent field
- the user's contribution depends on a cross-field bridge
