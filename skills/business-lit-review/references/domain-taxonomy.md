# Business Domain Taxonomy

## Accounting Topics

- disclosure, reporting quality, earnings management
- auditing, audit quality, auditor behavior
- tax avoidance and tax enforcement
- corporate governance and internal controls
- financial reporting regulation
- ESG disclosure and assurance
- analyst forecasts and capital-market consequences

## Finance Topics

- corporate finance, capital structure, payout, investment
- asset pricing, market microstructure, liquidity
- banking, credit, lending, financial intermediation
- household finance
- fintech and digital assets
- institutional investors and ownership
- risk management and derivatives

## Management Topics

- strategy, innovation, entrepreneurship
- organizations, teams, incentives, leadership
- platforms and digital transformation
- operations and supply chains
- international business
- human capital and labor inside firms

## Economics Topics

- industrial organization
- labor and personnel economics
- public economics and regulation
- political economy
- development, trade, and macro-finance

## Research Design Tags

- panel fixed effects
- difference-in-differences
- staggered adoption
- event study
- instrumental variables
- regression discontinuity
- matching
- survey experiment
- lab or field experiment
- textual analysis
- machine-learning measurement

## Boundary Guidance

Use the business workflow when the central contribution is to a business, accounting, finance, management, economics, or organizational literature.

Use the ML workflow only when the central contribution is an algorithm, benchmark, model, or computational method evaluated mainly by predictive performance.
