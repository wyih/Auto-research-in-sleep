# Business Novelty Check

## Verdict

`VERDICT: TOY_REPLICATION`

## Closest Existing Work

The idea closely follows standard CAPM and Fama-French teaching examples. Industry portfolio data from the Ken French Data Library are widely used for replication, instruction, and benchmark asset-pricing exercises.

## Novelty Risk

This project should not be framed as a novel finance contribution. The novelty is in testing the ARIS business workflow with a fully runnable public-data example.

## Safe Framing

- Workflow test
- Reproducible R example
- Public-data empirical toy project
- Claim discipline demonstration

## Risky Framing To Avoid

- New asset-pricing anomaly
- Causal effect of industry membership
- Evidence that one industry earns abnormal returns in a publishable sense

## Decision

Proceed as a toy project for skill validation.
